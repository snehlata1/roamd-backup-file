ROAMD was created by doctors for doctors and their patients. It is led by some of the most respected physicians in concierge and membership medicine.

Jordan Shlain, M.D.

Private Medical
San Francisco, CA

Jordan Shlain, M.D.

Private Medical | San Francisco, CA Dr. Jordan Shlain is the founder and managing partner of Private Medical, a referral-only private practice office in San Francisco, Silicon Valley and Los Angeles established in 2002. Private Medical was recently highlighted on the front page of the New York Times Business section as the best-in-class medical practice for individuals and families that value health as their most valuable asset. Their twenty-one physicians integrate internal medicine, pediatrics, naturopathic & gynecologic medicine to ensure a highly personal medical experience that emphasizes proactive prevention, surveillance and rapid response teams during crises both big and small. Dr. Shlain is an active advisor to many Silicon Valley companies that help solve real problems for real doctors.

Matt Priddy, M.D.

Priority Physicians
Indianapolis, IN

Matt Priddy, M.D.

Priority Physicians Indianapolis, IN Dr. Matthew Priddy has been practicing private medicine since 2003, and is the President of Indianapolis-based Priority Physicians. Priority Physicians is one of the largest private medicine practices in the Midwest with two locations and seven physicians currently. He has served multiple roles nationally in the American Academy of Private Physicians, and served as its President from 2013-2015. He is currently active with the Direct Primary Care Coalition and has traveled to Washington on multiple occasions to lobby for legislation that encourages alternative payment models in the delivery of primary care. Dr. Priddy is also a member of the AAFP, Indiana State Medical Society, and Indianapolis Medical Society. He serves on the Indianapolis board for the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society and was named their Man of the Year award recipient in 2014. Dr. Priddy attended Indiana University in Bloomington, Indiana before moving 60 miles north to attend the Indiana University School of Medicine in Indianapolis. He completed his training in Family Medicine at St. Vincent hospital, where he served as chief resident in his final year.

Colorado Center of Medical Excellence Denver, CO Dr. Cari Dawson has practiced Internal Medicine in Denver, Colorado since 2001 and in 2016 co-founded the Colorado Center of Medical Excellence. Signature Healthcare Charlotte, NC Dr. Jordan D. Lipton is a practicing primary care physician, entrepreneur, writer, speaker, and community leader. Radley Griffin, M.D.

Griffin Concierge Medical
Tampa, F

Radley Griffin, M.D.

Griffin Concierge Medical Tampa, F Dr. Radley Griffin, a board-certified family physician, is the founder and CEO of Griffin Concierge Medical in Tampa, Florida. After studying nutrition at the University of Florida, he attended Saba University School of Medicine. He completed his Family Medicine residency at the University of Florida where he was awarded the Physician Teacher of the Year. A passion for education has carried over to his private practice where he focuses on preventive medicine and the utilization of nutrition to reverse metabolic disease. Dr. Griffin enjoys collaborating with his colleagues in private medicine to promote best practices and to expand upon what he considers the only way to deliver primary care. Dr. Griffin served on the board of the American Academy of Private Physicians from 2014 to 2017. Whether educating established physicians who are interested in transitioning to private medicine or residents who are navigating their future employment options, Dr. Griffin is dedicated to growing the network of like-minded physicians who are focused on the delivery of high-quality care, patient quality of life and physician satisfaction.
